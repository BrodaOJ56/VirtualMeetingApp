Getting Started

To get started with the Virtual App:

  1. Ensure your camera and microphone are enabled.
  2. Click the "Share Screen" button to start sharing your screen.
  3. Interact with the host and other participants using the provided chat and video features.

Features

  • High-quality video conferencing and screen sharing.
  • Real-time chat for communicating with participants.
  • Automatic adjustment of video quality based on network conditions.

Guidelines

For the best experience in the Virtual App:

  • Use a stable internet connection to ensure clear audio and video.
  • Keep your microphone muted when not speaking to reduce background noise.
  • Be respectful and follow the host's guidelines during the virtual session.
